Our Data Products Storylane has been given a revamp! TAKE THE TOUR Data Products are highly trusted, re-usable, and consumable data assets. Data elements such as raw data, transformations, data quality rules, contracts, access patterns, and infrastructure have been organized into a single cohesive unit to align with specific requirements and objectives of a business to create a Data Product.
