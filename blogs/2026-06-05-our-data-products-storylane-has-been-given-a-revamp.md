Qlik has redesigned its Data Products Storylane, an interactive demo for the Data Products feature that combines raw data, transformations, quality rules, and infrastructure into reusable data assets. The revamped Storylane showcases the Qlik Trust Score across seven quality metrics and highlights Data Product Lineage capabilities for tracking data origins across analytics assets. A Data Marketplace is also featured, enabling discovery and sharing of curated data products within an organization.
